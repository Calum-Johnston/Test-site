About Us

Durham University Boxing Club is one of the most dedicated clubs in Durham. We are a small but growing club, with over 200 people attending weekly fitness sessions, and over 20 students competing for the uni.

If you'd like to get involved, we train everyday 6-8pm at Hild Bede East Gym. Feel free to come along and test it out, we offer 5 free sessions to any beginners who want to try us out!
